Step into a world of aristocratic opulence with Royal Desire. Saffron Sandal (Eau de Parfum, 50 ml)

For Price Contact Us

Saffron Sandal marries the world’s most coveted spice with creamy woods for a scent that’s both exotic and cocooning. White Oud (Eau de Parfum, 50 ml)

For Price Contact Us

A modern, ethereal take on the classic oud fragrance, White Oud opens with delicate top notes of neroli and freesia over a backdrop of crisp green apple. Beneath the bright facade lies a heart of radiant white oud accord blended with creamy sandalwood and a hint of sheer jasmine petals. In the final phase, soft powdery musk and warm cashmere wood lend a velvety, almost skin-like finish that lingers for hours. White Oud is perfect for both day and evening wear—an understated yet sophisticated scent that whispers luxury rather than shouting it.
